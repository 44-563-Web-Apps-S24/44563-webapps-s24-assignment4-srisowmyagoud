SOLENI.

[Enters with CHEBUTIKIN from the dining-room]

With one hand I can only lift fifty-four pounds, but with both hands I can lift 180, or even 200 pounds. From this I conclude that two men are not twice as strong as one, but three times, perhaps even more....

CHEBUTIKIN.
.

If your hair is coming out... take an ounce of naphthaline and hail a bottle of spirit... dissolve and use daily....

When found make a note of! Not that I want it though....

[Crosses it out]

It doesn't matter.

IRINA.

Ivan Romanovitch, dear Ivan Romanovitch!
